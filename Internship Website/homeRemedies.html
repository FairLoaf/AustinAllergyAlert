Home Remedies





NETI POT: A Neti Pot is a small teapot that can help you flush out your nasal passage. Doing this can help you breathe better by unclogging your nose, a common allergy symptom. Neti Pots can also remove backed-up mucus, relieve sinus pressure, and fully rinse the nasal passage.



AIR FILTERS: Air filters can help remove airborne impurities in the air. It is beneficial to have these in your car or daily living spaces as they can filter several airborne allergies. They additionally remove dust and bacteria from the air.



WARM SHOWERS (THROUOGHLY WASH HAIR AND BODY): Warm showers can help you get rid of several allergens that may have stuck to your clothes or hair throughout the day. They can also help your body relax and ease common allergy symptoms such as congestion.



FREQUENTLY WASH SHEETS AND LAUNDRY: It's extremely beneficial to frequently wash your sheets and do your laundry during allergy season as it prevents the build-up of allergens in your daily living space. It can also help you maintain sanitary practices during allergy seasons or times when your allergen is high.



KEEP WINDOWS CLOSED: Keeping your windows closed throughout the allergy season can help prevent allergens from coming inside of your daily living space and can help you avoid exposure to your allergen.



WEAR A MASK IN HIGH-ALLERGEN LOCATIONS: Although you should try your best to avoid places with a large amount of your allergen or completely avoid going out on days where your allergen is very present, sometimes it is unavoidable. During these days wearing a mask can help you avoid breaking in your allergen.
